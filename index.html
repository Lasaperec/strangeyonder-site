Strange Yonder is an independent artist management label that curates International and National tours throughout Australia & runs a record label ‘La Sape Records’. "Lovers of landscape; brought on by sound, with the idea of becoming a presence at every level of creation. Beginning as a hobby; born out of necessity to get involved, Strange Yonder has grown into an entity that consumes time, triggers frustration, breaks hearts, gives boners, pushes boundaries and deeply satisfies".
